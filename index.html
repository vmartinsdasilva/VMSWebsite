Muvuca | Restaurant Branding

Muvuca is a Brazilian Bar and Club open for everyone who wishes to have a good time enjoying good food while being served by our friendly staff. We believe that even if living far from our homeland we can still serve the true “Brazilian way”.

Logo Equation

Brazil is a whirlwind of culture and creativity! Our roots are a vibrant mix of influences from all over the globe, and that's what makes us so unique. Native Brazilians are legends when it comes to crafting beautiful vases and home decor, and the patterns you see on these pieces are where the magic all starts!

Then there's Capim Dourado—aka "Golden Grass"—a stunning, shimmering plant found only in the Brazilian savanna. It's like nature's glitter, and it’s used to make everything from baskets to placemats to jewelry. Talk about natural beauty!

And let’s not forget muvuca—a Portuguese word that means a lively, noisy crowd that’s full of energy and excitement. Sounds like a perfect description of us Brazilians, right? Always buzzing, always full of life!
